Enterprise Earth release ‘Sleep Is For The Dead’

By Matthew Young

Not ones to sit around after a release, techy death metallers Enterprise Earth are back after what seems like no time at all with a new single ahead of their new release entitled ‘Luciferous’. Set for the release on the 5th of April, ‘Luciferous’ will be the bands third full length album and will be released via eOne/Good Fight Music.

Smashing your earbuds with their unique brand of darkness, Enterprise Earth had this to say of the new album. ‘The evil we perceive in this world is a mere reflection of our inner darkness. We are the architects of evil as it is projected upon the landscape of our reality. The Devil exists. He exists in us all’. Continuing, ‘After following our natural progressions as musicians and as people, ‘Luciferous’ came to life. It’s our strongest, most mature release to date and signifies a rebirth while retaining all of the elements of our past. We’re extremely proud of this release and can’t wait to unleash it upon the world’.

In terms of the new single, Vocalist Dan Watson has explained that ‘’Sleep is for the Dead’ is about digging deep inside myself when the world turns it’s back on me’. It’s about peeling off the exterior shell that our society plastered on the outside and awakening the true inner version of myself rather than suppress it to fit into the cookie cutter mold that society tries to pressure us to fit into. I would rather be awake and face the pain than be asleep and suppress it’.

While you wait on bated breath for ‘Luciferous’ you can catch the band performing their new track in the video below.
